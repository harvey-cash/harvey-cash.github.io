Health, Death, and Resting

HP & Mortality

Hit Points Max HP = (8 + CON) × Level

Worn Items do not contribute.

  • Downed (0 HP): Roll a d20 Death Save (DC 10) at the start of your turn.
  • ✓ 3 Successes: Wake up at 1 HP, gain 1 Exhaustion.
  • ✗ 3 Failures: Die permanently.

Exhaustion

Die permanently upon reaching 3 levels of exhaustion.

While exhausted, you have disadvantage on all Ability rolls, and your Max HP is reduced by 1 per level.

Recovery

Short Rest (30+ mins)

Heal 1d6 + CON HP. If exhausted, pass a CON DC 10 check to lose 1 level of exhaustion.

Long Rest (6+ hours)

Fully restore HP and remove all levels of exhaustion. Restore all spell slots.

Items and Equipment

Carry unlimited items in your backpack, but you can only equip: 3 Trinkets, 2 Weapons, and 1 Outfit.

Trinkets Max 3

Grant passive benefits, situational advantages, or static modifier bonuses.

Outfits Max 1

Set your flat Armor Class (AC). Cannot be equipped or removed in combat.

Unarmored = 10
Light = 12
Medium = 14
Heavy = 16

Weapons Max 2

Unarmed strikes deal 1d4 + STR. Typical weapons deal 1d6 + Modifier.

  • Melee: STR vs AC (5ft).
  • Ranged: DEX vs AC (disadv. within 5ft or beyond 50ft).

Mechanics

  • Cantrips: Simple magic. Find a Cantrip Scroll once, read it, and you can cast it for free forever without slots.
  • Leveled Spells: Complex magic. During a rest, prepare a total number of spells up to your character Level. Prepared scrolls must be on your body; unprepared in your backpack. All revert to unprepared on resting.
  • Concentration: You can only concentrate on one spell at a time.

Resolution

  • Caster Rolls (Attacks): Use the listed Ability vs. target AC (or a GM set DC).
  • Target Rolls (Saves): Use the listed Ability vs. the Caster's DC (10 + caster's matching modifier).

Turns and Combat

Turns represent 6 seconds of real time. On your turn, you get:

1 Action 1 Object Interaction 30ft Movement

Actions

  • Attacking
  • Casting a spell
  • Interacting with creatures
  • Changing an equipped weapon/trinket

Object Interactions

  • Retrieving/stowing a backpack item
  • Opening doors
  • Pulling levers

Initiative

Roll a DEX check when combat begins.

Critical Damage

Double the number of damage dice rolled on a Nat 20 (weapons & spells).

Cover

Partial cover grants a flat +2 bonus to AC.

Surprise

Initiate surprise via contested Ability checks (e.g., Attacker's active stat vs. Target's DEX, at GM discretion).

Restrictions

Already-engaged creatures cannot be surprised. Unconscious targets are surprised automatically.

Effects

Surprised targets roll initiative with disadvantage but join immediately. Attacks against them have advantage. Expires end of round 1.

Basic Items

Weapons

  • Sword: Melee 1d6 + STR. +1 STR, -1 WIS
  • Dagger: Melee 1d6 + STR/DEX. Ranged 1d4 + DEX. Lost when thrown.
  • Warhammer: Melee 1d8 + STR. -1 DEX, +1 CHA on intimidation checks.
  • Crossbow: Ranged 1d6 + DEX.

Outfits

  • Robes: AC 10. +2 INT, -1 WIS
  • Leathers: AC 12. +1 WIS
  • Chainmail: AC 14. -1 CHA
  • Plate: AC 16. -1 DEX, +1 CON, disadvantage on DEX sneak checks.

Trinkets

  • Shield: AC +2, -1 DEX, +1 WIS
  • Ruffian's Cloak: +1 DEX, -1 CHA
  • Tiny Dictator: +2 INT, advantage against bureaucrats.
  • Esoteric Lockpick: +1 DEX, -1 WIS, advantage on lockpicking.

Consumables

  • Healing Potion: 2d4+2 HP
  • Supreme Healing Potion: Fall unconscious for 1 turn. Then, heal 10d4+10 HP.
  • Shard of Divinity: Restore one spell slot.

Basic Spells

Cantrips (Free to cast once learned)

Dancing Light: Create one hovering, torch-sized light which follows you. Lasts 1 hour.

Druidcraft: Predict the weather, make a flower bloom, or instantly light/snuff a campfire.

Mage Hand: Create a spectral hand that can manipulate objects or carry up to 10 lbs within 30ft.

Thaumaturgy: Create harmless sensory effects (booming voice, flickering flames, tremors in the floor).

1st Level Spells

Bless: Conc. Choose up to 3 creatures. For 10 mins, they roll an extra 1d4 on all attacks and Ability checks.

Charm Person: Conc. Target rolls WIS vs. your CHA. Fail: they treat you as friendly for 1 hour.

Comprehend Languages: Conc. Understand all spoken/written languages encountered for 1 hour.

Cure Wounds: Touch a creature to instantly heal them for 1d8 + your WIS.

Detect Alignment: Sense the moral alignment of one creature.

Divine Hex: Conc. Target rolls WIS vs CHA. Fail: deal extra 1d4 damage when you hit them. 1 hour.

Grease: Coat a 10ft square in slick grease, lasting 10 mins. Creatures entering/starting turn must pass DEX DC10 or fall prone.

Inflict Wounds: Melee spell attack. 1d10 + INT

Eldritch Blast: Ranged spell attack. 1d8 + INT

Protection from E/G/E: Conc. Touch creature. For 10 mins, otherworldly entities have disadvantage attacking them.

Shield of Faith: Conc. Grant a creature +2 AC for 10 minutes.

Speak with Animals: Conc. Communicate clearly with beasts for 10 minutes.

2nd Level Spells

Barkskin: Conc. Touch a creature. Skin hardens, gaining resistance to physical damage for 10 minutes.

Detect Secrets: Magically sense the presence of hidden doors, traps, or compartments within 30ft.

Enhance Ability: Conc. Touch a creature. Gain advantage on Ability checks for one chosen stat for 10 minutes.

Knock: Instantly unlock a mundane or magical lock. Emits a loud ring heard up to 300ft away.

Prayer of Healing: Takes 10 mins to cast. Up to 6 creatures within 30ft heal 2d8 + your CHA.

Speak with Plants: Conc. Communicate with plants for 10 minutes, commanding them to part or entangle.

3rd Level Spells

Aura of Vitality: Conc. For 1 minute, on the start of your turn, allies within 15ft heal 2d4 + your CON.

Revivify: Sacrifice a diamond. Touch a creature dead < 1 hour. Return to life with 1 HP + 1 Exhaustion.

Speak with Dead: Conc. Ask a corpse up to 5 questions. It knows only what it knew in life.

Turn Undead: Undead within 30ft roll CHA vs your WIS. Fail: must spend turns running away for 1 minute.
